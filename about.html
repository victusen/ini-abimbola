Biography

The Story Behind
the Work

I bring over 25 years of leadership across public and private sectors, advising on institutional and development innovation, strategy development and deployment, public policy execution, stakeholder engagement, external/public relations, sustainability, ESG, and corporate/board governance—often in complex environments where delivery depends on disciplined governance and credible stakeholder alignment.

With 15+ years in development sector program management, I specialise in innovation, Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E), and Results-Based Monitoring & Evaluation (RBME). My expertise lies in helping governments, public agencies, international development agencies, not-for-profits, and corporate boards fix governance and execution breakdowns such as unclear decision rights, weak accountability, stakeholder gridlock, and failed strategies.

As a 2008 Stanford Draper Hills Fellow and Founder/CEO of Thistle Group (consulting and gas interests), I serve on multiple boards including the Mirabel Centre, bringing deep expertise in Nigerian and state government reforms, election oversight — having managed district elections for fair outcomes — and global development initiatives.

I recently earned my DBA in Strategic Management from Rome Business School (2025), further deepening my capacity to architect transformative solutions at the intersection of governance, strategy, and institutional performance.
