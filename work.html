Expertise Areas

What Ini Brings to the Table

01
Core Expertise

Institutional Reform & Delivery Systems

Ini architects decision rights, KPIs, and innovation performance routines that bridge the gap between strategy and execution. In environments where delivery depends on clarity of mandate and accountability across silos, she designs the governance operating model that makes it possible. Her work here spans regional public agencies, state governments, and multilateral development programmes — ensuring that reform doesn't just get written, it gets implemented.

02
Core Expertise

Corporate & Board Governance

Ini establishes sustainability and ESG structures, committee charters, reporting frameworks, and oversight routines that strengthen board effectiveness, regulatory compliance, and long-term organisational resilience. Her board advisory work has covered committee design, director development, ESG reporting integration, and the structural changes needed to shift boards from reactive oversight to proactive stewardship.

03
Core Expertise

Performance Intelligence & M&E

With 15+ years in development sector programme management, Ini designs M&E frameworks, impact measurement systems, and adaptive learning loops for donor-funded and large-scale public projects. She transforms data into actionable insights for continuous improvement — enabling real-time course correction and evidence-based decision-making for governments, NGOs, and international development agencies.

04
Core Expertise

Public Policy & Strategy Execution

Navigating high-stakes stakeholder environments through credible, transparent performance and report tracking — Ini ensures policy initiatives translate into tangible outcomes with sustained political and public support. Her work in communications strategy, stakeholder alignment, and policy deployment has covered 11+ Ministries, Departments and Agencies, reaching 18 local government areas with unified strategic messaging.
